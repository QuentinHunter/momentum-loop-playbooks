## TROUBLESHOOTING

### Problem #1: Low Welcome Sequence Engagement

**Symptoms:**
- Email 1 open rate < 40%
- Email 2-7 open rates declining to < 20%
- No replies, no clicks

**Diagnosis:**
1. **Spam folder issues** (check Mail-Tester.com score)
2. **Subject lines too generic** (test curiosity vs. clarity)
3. **Wrong audience** (junk traffic from ads?)

**Fix:**
- Clean list (remove hard bounces)
- A/B test subject lines (5 variants)
- Add "whitelist" instruction in Email 1 ("Mark this as Not Spam")
- Use reply-to address (not no-reply@) to improve deliverability

---

### Problem #2: Scoring Doesn't Predict Buying

**Symptoms:**
- Hot leads (80+ points) don't convert
- Cold leads (< 20 points) are buying
- Sales says "These aren't qualified"

**Diagnosis:**
- Scoring weights wrong (valuing wrong behaviors)
- Missing fit criteria (behavioral score without ICP check)
- Time decay not working (old activity inflates scores)

**Fix:**
- Review closed deals: What did buyers DO before buying?
- Adjust weights (e.g., pricing page visit worth more than email open)
- Add fit multipliers (perfect ICP = 1.5x score boost)
- Implement time decay (30-day inactivity = -50% score)
- Recalibrate quarterly (buying patterns change)

---

### Problem #3: Trigger Campaigns Get Low Response

**Symptoms:**
- Trigger emails < 10% reply rate
- Feels automated/generic
- Timing feels off

**Diagnosis:**
- Messaging too robotic (obvious template)
- Timing wrong (too fast or too slow)
- CTA unclear (what do they do next?)

**Fix:**
- Add personalization beyond name (reference specific behavior in detail)
- Test send delays (instant vs. 15 min vs. 1 hour vs. next day)
- Use conversational tone (write like you're texting a colleague)
- Clear binary CTAs ("Reply YES or NO" vs. "Let us know your thoughts")

---

### Problem #4: Leads Stall at Warm Stage (30-50 Points)

**Symptoms:**
- 40-50% of leads stuck at 30-50 points
- Engage with content but never reach hot
- Long cycles (60+ days, no progression)

**Diagnosis:**
- No momentum mechanisms (passive nurture only)
- Missing engagement ladder (big gap between email click â†’ call booking)
- Lack of urgency (no reason to act now)

**Fix:**
- Add micro-commitments (reply to email â†’ watch video â†’ take 2nd quiz â†’ book call)
- Introduce time-limited offers (expires Friday)
- Run live events (webinars create deadlines)
- Use multi-touch acceleration (email + SMS + LinkedIn for 50+ scorers)

---

### Problem #5: High Unsubscribe Rate (> 1% Per Email)

**Symptoms:**
- Unsubscribes climbing
- Spam complaints
- List shrinking faster than growing

**Diagnosis:**
- Frequency too high (daily emails = burnout)
- Relevance too low (generic content not specific to accountancy)
- Expectation mismatch (they expected X, got Y)

**Fix:**
- Segment by engagement (send more to engaged, less to cold)
- Let leads choose frequency (weekly digest option)
- Deliver on promise (if quiz was about growth bottlenecks, nurture about bottlenecks)
- Use sunset policy (remove 90-day inactive = cleaner list, better deliverability)
