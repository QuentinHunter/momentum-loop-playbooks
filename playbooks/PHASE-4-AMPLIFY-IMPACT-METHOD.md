## COMPONENT 4: ACCELERATION (Quick Wins & Early Momentum)

### What It Is
Delivering tangible results in the first 7-30 days to build confidence and reduce buyer's remorse.

### Why It Matters
Clients who see early wins stay engaged. Those who don't get results fast churn or demand refunds.

### The Quick-Win Formula

**Quick Win = (Low Effort + Fast Results) √ó High Visibility**

#### **Week 1 Quick Win: The Low-Hanging Fruit**

**Goal:** Prove value within 7 days

**Examples:**
1. **ATTRACT:** Get first 10 leads from existing audience
   - How: Email your list with Catalyst Score‚Ñ¢ quiz
   - Time: 2 hours (write email, send, promote)

2. **ACTIVATE:** Increase email open rates 15%+

**Key:** These wins require minimal effort but create instant momentum.

#### **Week 2-4: The Compound Wins**

**Goal:** Stack small wins into bigger results

**Examples:**
1. **Lead Gen Flywheel:** 
   - Week 1: 10 leads
   - Week 2: 20 leads (systems improving)
   - Week 3: 35 leads (compounding)

   - Week 1: 10% conversion (baseline)
