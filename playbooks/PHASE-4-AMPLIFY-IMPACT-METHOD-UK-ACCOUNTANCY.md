## TROUBLESHOOTING

### Problem #1: Client Not Seeing Results (Month 1-2)

**Symptoms:**
- < 10 leads in Month 1
- No discovery calls booked
- Client anxious ("Is this working?")

**Diagnosis:**
- **Traffic issue:** Ads not spending, organic reach too low
- **Conversion issue:** Quiz conversion < 10% (messaging off)
- **ICP issue:** Leads coming in, but wrong fit (not actionable)

**Fix:**
- **Traffic:** Increase ad budget, add more channels (organic content frequency)
- **Conversion:** A/B test quiz headlines, simplify questions, improve landing page
- **ICP:** Narrow targeting (LinkedIn job titles, industries)
