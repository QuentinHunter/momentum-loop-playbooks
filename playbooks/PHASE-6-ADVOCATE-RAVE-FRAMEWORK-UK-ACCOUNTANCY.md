## TROUBLESHOOTING

### Problem #1: Low Referral Rate (< 10% of Clients Refer)

**Symptoms:**
- Sent 50 referral requests, got 3 referrals
- Clients love you (NPS >60) but don't refer
- No system in place

**Diagnosis:**
- **Timing wrong:** Asking too early (no results yet) or too late (forgot)
- **Too vague:** "Let me know if you know anyone" (not specific)
- **No incentive:** Clients don't know there's a reward

**Fix:**
- Ask at 7 milestones (not random)
- Make it specific: "Know any practice owners in [City]?"
- Promote rewards: "Refer 1 = Â£500 credit" (in every request)

---

### Problem #2: Referrals Don't Convert (Low Close Rate)

**Symptoms:**
- Getting referrals, but they don't book calls or sign up
- Conversion rate < 30%

**Diagnosis:**
- **Weak intro:** Client sends link with no context ("Check this out")
- **Cold referral:** Referred person doesn't know/trust referrer
- **Poor follow-up:** You didn't reach out fast enough

**Fix:**
- Template intros (add context: "I've been working with them for 6 months, got great results...")
- Qualify referrals: Ask client "How well do you know them?" (close friend = warm, LinkedIn connection = cold)
- Speed to contact: Call referred lead within 24 hours (strike while iron is hot)

---

### Problem #3: Clients Stop Referring After First One

**Symptoms:**
- Client refers 1 person, then never again
- No repeat referrers

**Diagnosis:**
- **Forgot:** No reminder system (out of sight, out of mind)
- **Used up network:** Referred their 1 close friend, don't know anyone else
- **Bad experience:** First referral had issues (client now hesitant)

**Fix:**
- **Quarterly reminders:** "Who else do you know?" (automated)
- **Expand network:** "Any peers from ICAEW events? LinkedIn connections?"
- **Ensure quality:** If referral had bad experience, FIX IT (then ask client to refer again)
